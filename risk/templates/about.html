Our Mission and Values

Our Mission
Individuals and organizations in every industry are facing continously increasing environmental risk and liability, resulting in a need to have higher quality environmental insurance. At Glasswing, we are commited to ensuring that consumers are able to find high quality and cost effective insurance policies for their properties. To do so, we crowdsource information in addition to our own data collection to provide estimations for insurance, resulting in transparent policies and prices. In addition, we work with insurance providers to ensure that their insurance policies remain competitive and are effective at protecting consumers from environmental damages. Together, we can work to create accessible quality and fairly priced insurance providers to protect you from the uncertainities of an everchanging environmental situation.
